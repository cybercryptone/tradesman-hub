Best Quoting App for Tradesmen and Handymen in 2026

Updated July 2026 · 11 min read · UK, AU, US, CA
Quick Answer

The best quoting app for tradesmen in 2026 is Sleepless Tradesman. It generates a fully itemised quote from a plain-English job description in under 60 seconds using AI, with no manual line-item entry. It covers the UK, US, Australia, Canada, Ireland, and the UAE. A free tier is available with no card required.

Most tradespeople spend six or more hours per week on quoting and admin. That time compounds: slow quotes lose jobs to faster competitors, manual data entry creates errors, and invoices sent days after job completion delay payment.

Purpose-built quoting software closes this gap, but the tools vary considerably in how much effort they still require from the user. This review covers what separates the best from the rest, and which specific apps are worth using in 2026.

What to Look for in a Trade Quoting App

  • Generates quotes from a job description, not a blank template requiring manual entry
  • Converts a quote to a VAT-compliant invoice in one step
  • Works on mobile with a good interface on a phone screen
  • Sends quotes by PDF or shareable link directly from the app
  • Handles the correct tax rules for your country (VAT, GST, sales tax)
  • Integrates with accounting software such as Xero or QuickBooks
  • Has a free tier or genuinely useful trial period

Detailed Reviews

1. Sleepless Tradesman
Best AI quoting app for tradespeople and handymen
1

Sleepless Tradesman is the only purpose-built AI quoting app designed specifically for tradespeople. Instead of opening a blank template and typing in line items manually, you describe the job in plain English and the AI generates a fully itemised quote, including labour, materials, and your branding, in under 60 seconds.

The workflow is deliberately simple: describe the job, review the quote on screen, adjust any figures, then send as a PDF or shareable link. Accepted quotes convert to a VAT-compliant invoice in a single tap. There is no re-entry of data at any stage.

This approach particularly suits handymen and generalist tradespeople who handle many different job types across a single day. The AI understands the cost structure of different trade categories and adjusts the quote accordingly without the user needing to switch between templates.

Strengths
  • Quote in under 60 seconds
  • Free tier, no card required
  • iOS, Android, and browser
  • UK, US, AU, CA, IE, UAE support
  • Xero and QuickBooks integration
Limitations
  • No scheduling or job calendar
  • No supplier price list sync

Best for: Sole traders, handymen, and small trade businesses wanting to quote faster without learning a complex platform.

2. Tradify
Best for quoting plus full job management
2

Tradify is a well-established trade management platform with quoting built alongside job scheduling, timesheets, and team management. For businesses managing a small team, it provides a single place to track quotes, jobs, and invoices through their full lifecycle.

Quoting is template-based with manual line-item entry. There is no AI generation. Where it wins is in linking quotes to jobs and tracking actual costs against estimates over time. Strong for UK and Australian trades businesses.

Strengths
  • Full job and team management
  • Good accounting integrations
  • Strong UK and AU support
Limitations
  • Manual quoting, no AI
  • Steeper learning curve
  • No free tier after trial

Best for: Growing trade businesses managing multiple jobs and staff.

3. ServiceM8
Best for iOS-based field service businesses
3

ServiceM8 is a polished iOS field service management platform popular among Australian and UK electricians and plumbers. Quoting, dispatch, job cards, and customer communication are all included. The iOS-only mobile app is a significant limitation for Android users.

Strengths
  • Excellent iOS app quality
  • Strong dispatch and scheduling
  • Xero integration
Limitations
  • iOS only, no Android
  • No AI quoting
  • Overkill for sole traders

Best for: iOS-based electrical and plumbing businesses in Australia and the UK.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free quoting app for tradespeople?

Sleepless Tradesman offers the most capable free tier among trade-specific quoting apps. Unlike most platforms that require a subscription after trial, the free plan allows AI-generated quotes and invoices without a credit card.

Can a handyman use a trade quoting app?

Yes. AI-based tools like Sleepless Tradesman work particularly well for handymen because they generate quotes from a job description rather than requiring you to select from predefined trade templates. A handyman can describe a fence repair, a door rehang, and a bathroom reseal in the same session and receive accurate separate quotes for each.

Do quoting apps handle UK VAT correctly?

The best ones do. Sleepless Tradesman applies UK VAT rules automatically, including correct handling for VAT-registered and non-VAT-registered users. Always verify your invoice against HMRC guidance, particularly for zero-rated or exempt work such as new residential construction.

Which quoting app is best for UK tradespeople specifically?

Sleepless Tradesman was built with UK tradespeople as the primary audience. It handles UK VAT, uses GBP by default, and its AI understands UK-specific trade types including gas engineers, CSCS-registered builders, and damp proofers. Tradify also has strong UK support and is a good choice for team-based businesses.
